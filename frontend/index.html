Core Capability Areas

The platform is split into specialized engines that work together under agentic control to handle ingestion, reasoning, and financial action execution.

📄 OCR Intelligence

Reads scanned statements and mixed-quality bank exports. Extracts dates, merchants, debit-credit direction, and amounts with validation checks to reduce noisy entries.

🤖 Agentic AI Framework

Task-aware routing decides which service should run next: parser, categorizer, anomaly detector, planner, or tool execution layer. This enables reliable multi-step automation.

🧠 AI Insight Engine

LLM reasoning turns raw analytics into plain-language insights: spending narratives, risk warnings, optimization opportunities, and prioritized next actions.

How OCR + Agentic AI Works

From document upload to guided action, each stage is explicit and traceable.

1) Capture and ParsePDF/image statements are processed with OCR and transaction fields are extracted into structured records.
2) Normalize and CleanMerchant labels, timestamps, and amount directions are standardized for consistent analytics quality.
3) Agentic RoutingAn orchestration layer chooses downstream services based on intent, confidence, and detected anomalies.
4) Insight and ActionLLM-backed advisors explain what changed, why it matters, and can trigger budgeting or planning updates.
