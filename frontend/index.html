How The Platform Works

Each step is deterministic + AI-guided: OCR ingestion creates clean records, agentic routing executes the right tools, and insight generation returns explainable recommendations.

1. Upload and OCRStatements and receipts are parsed for dates, merchants, amount, debit or credit type, and metadata.
2. Normalize and CategorizeMerchant aliases are merged, ambiguous text is cleaned, and categories are assigned for consistent dashboards.
3. Agentic Decision RoutingFramework selects downstream tools: anomaly checks, recurring detection, cash flow forecast, budget optimizer.
4. AI Insight and RecommendationsLLM layer explains trends and proposes concrete next actions with rationale and expected impact.
